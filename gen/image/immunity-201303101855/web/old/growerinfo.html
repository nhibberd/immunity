Grower Information

Information provided below for growers in regards to Contracts, Payment and Ginning

//Contracts for Raw Cotton

AgPro Services offer a choice of contracts for growers selling raw cotton.

Terms and conditions for these contracts are available below:

  • X contract
  • Y contract
  • Z contract

All contracts are for 227 kg bales.

Cotton lint is purchased as a Based Middling Colour, 3 leaf, 3.8 – 4.5 micronaire grade, and FOB Gin yard. Cotton lint not of this grade is subject to a premium and discount payment schedule.

For more information and advice on marketing your cotton contact the AgPro Services team.

//Payment

A number of payment term options exist for fixed price contracts.

  • 14 days after ginning
  • 100 percent July
  • July/September/December split payment

Payments are made in Australian dollars or US dollars plus GST.

//Ginning of Raw Cotton

AgPro Services offers two forms of ginning contracts.

  • Lint only – gross ginning
  • Gin lint for cotton seed

Terms and conditions for these contracts are available below:

  • AB contract t & c’s
  • CD contract t & c’s

For more information on ginning your cotton contact the AgPro Services team.
