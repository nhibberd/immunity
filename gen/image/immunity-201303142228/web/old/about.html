About

//AgPro Services Pty Ltd

AgPro Services was established by Cathy Cook in 2010 following the sale and subsequent split of the Dunavant Group assets in Australia. Representing long established global commodity companies Louis Dreyfus Commodities and Dunvant Ginning, AgPro Services is a major service provider of commodity marketing, brokerage and cotton ginning services to Australian farmers. With decades of industry experience, the AgPro Services team offers a personalised service founded on knowledge, understanding and respect.

“Our mission is to provide you with effective and reliable solutions for the marketing and ginning of your cotton.”

Cathy Cook
