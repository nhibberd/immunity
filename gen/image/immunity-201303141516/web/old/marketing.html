Marketing

AgPro Services partner with leading commodity trader Louis Dreyfus Commodities to market, classify, store and export Australian raw cotton and other commodities worldwide

//Louis Dreyfus Commodities

Established in 1851, Louis Dreyfus Commodities is one of the leading worldwide trader of agricultural commodities and a global leader in cotton merchandising.

In 2010 Louis Dreyfus acquired the Dunavant marketing and warehousing businesses which typically handle approximately 20 percent of Australian production annually.

AgPro Services is a provider of all grower services activity on behalf of Louis Dreyfus Commodities in Australia.
