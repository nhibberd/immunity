Ginning

The AgPro Services team is your point of contact for ginning contracts and processing requirements.

//Dunavant Ginning

Dunavant Ginning has operated in Australia since 1987. In 2010 the Dunavant family sold the ginning businesses to Geocoton Copaco. With over 20 years experience processing Australian Cotton, Dunavant Ginning have earned a solid repuation as a quality operator.

//Ginning Your Cotton

Agpro Services offer ginning services for cotton growers to process seed cotton into baled lint. Ginning services are provided in the Gwydir Valley, Darling Downs and Central Queensland in agreement with Dunavant Ginning.

Dunavant Ginning operates three gins on the eastern seaboard – a five stand Gin outside of Emerald, and four stand Gins in both Dalby and Moree.

The Agpro Services team is your point of contact for all ginning contracts and processing requirements.
